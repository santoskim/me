How it started

Before..

I have been using Excel ever since I was young. I remember creating spreadsheets for various hobbies, such as tracking my fitness progress or researching food places in new cities. I would input my data and then use Excel to create graphs that allowed me to visually see the impact of different actions. For example, I could compare how my fitness routine affected my weight over time, or analyze the ratings of different food places to find the best ones. Looking back, I now realize that these early experiences were my introduction to data analytics.

.. and then..

My career in mind was completely different. I majored in biological sciences and planned to pursue a career in nursing. All it took was a class about statistics on data analysis to make a significant impact on me. I always looked forward to attend class and completing assignments because I enjoyed coding and creating visualizations with RStudio. This experience made me passionate for data analytics, and the idea of manipulating data, solving problems and creating visualizations to communicate insights excites me.

.. now

I am highly motivated to continue pursuing my career in data analytics. Each new project and connection I make in the field helps me learn and grow, which fuels my passion even further.


To me, data is about..

Collaboration

A group of diverse backgrounds and expertise can uncover valuable insights that couldn’t be done alone. The combination of each individuals’ unique skills and knowledge can lead to more innovative solutions and better decision-making.

Critical Thinking

Critical thinking is a foundation for effective data analysis. By approaching problems from alternative perspectives, identifying potential issues, and striving for accuracy, we can ensure the reliability and actionability of our insights.

Creativity

Approaching problems from different angles can uncover data that may not have been immediately apparent, leading to new insights and opportunities. A creative approach to analyzing data can also lead to more effective visualizations and storytelling, helping to communicate complex information in an engaging and easily understandable way.
